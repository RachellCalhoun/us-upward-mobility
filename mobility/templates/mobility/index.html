Summary

To measure how upwardly mobile a community is 8 domains to consider: Housing, Health, Local Governance, Neighborhoods, Saftey, Education, Work and Financial Wellbeing. Each of these domains have a number of metrics collected from various datasets with the aim to investigate not only how upwardly mobile a community is but also in which areas that community could focus their attention and resources. This project was inspired and has intended to follow the research report Boosting Upward Mobility: metrics to Inform Local Action.
